Recall that economics is the study of how a society decides what gets produced, how it gets produced, and who gets what. On the other hand, finance is the study of the financial or monetary aspects of production, spending, borrowing, and lending decisions. Finance deals with raising and using of money by individuals, firms, government and foreign investors. At a macro level, finance is concerned with how the financial systems coordinates and channels the flow of funds from lenders to borrowers and vice versa.
